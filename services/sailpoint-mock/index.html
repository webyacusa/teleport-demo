Trigger a New Access Approval

How this works

In production, an employee would log in to SailPoint's "My Access" portal and request a role. Once approved by governance policy, SailPoint fires an outbound webhook to BAMOE with the employee + role payload. This page simulates that approval event. Pick an employee and role, click the button, and watch BAMOE pick up the workflow.
